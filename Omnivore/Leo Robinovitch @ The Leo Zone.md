[The Scout Mindset](https:&#x2F;&#x2F;en.wikipedia.org&#x2F;wiki&#x2F;The%5FScout%5FMindset) is a book by [Julia Galef](https:&#x2F;&#x2F;juliagalef.com&#x2F;). The book gives practical techniques for testing how evidence-driven we are being in a given moment, for example, probing for double standards in your views or asking yourself what an unbiased outsider would think. One of the more powerful sections for me talked about identity. Ideas can become part of your identity, and then updating your view on something requires also updating a view about yourself. The book also discusses the relationship between identity and activism.
