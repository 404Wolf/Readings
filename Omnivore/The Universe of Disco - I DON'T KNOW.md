[I DON&#39;T KNOW](https:&#x2F;&#x2F;blog.plover.com&#x2F;brain&#x2F;i-dont-know.html)   

If you&#39;re an annoying know-it-all like me, I suggest that you try playing the following game when you attend a conference or a user group meetup or even a work meeting. The game is:

&gt; If someone asks you a question, and you say “I don&#39;t know”, you score a point.

That&#39;s it. That&#39;s the game. “I don&#39;t know” doesn&#39;t have to be perfectly truthful, only approximately truthful.

I forgot, there is one other rule:

&gt; If you follow up with something like “But if I had to guess…” you lose your point again.
