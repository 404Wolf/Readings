[Loki the comedian](https:&#x2F;&#x2F;blog.plover.com&#x2F;misc&#x2F;goat-scrote.html)   

Toph and I were discussing the story of Loki and Skaði, one of my favorites. ([Previously](https:&#x2F;&#x2F;blog.plover.com&#x2F;lang&#x2F;norse-scrotum.html).) The Æsir have killed Skaði&#39;s father, and owe her compensation. She has been sad since her father died, she says, and demands that the Æsir make her laugh. Loki rubs his hands together and says &quot;Leave this to me!&quot;.

He takes a rope, ties one end to a goat&#39;s beard, and the other and to his scrotum. Hilarity ensues.

Skaði tries not to laugh. She fails.

Toph asked a question I had not thought of, but that has been in my head ever since: &quot;Do you think it was an idea he thought up on the spur of the moment? Or was it a bit he had planned ahead of time?&quot;

Wow, I don&#39;t know. Was Loki suddenly struck with brilliant inspiration? Or did he think &#39;Aha, I knew this idea would come in handy sooner or later!&#39; They&#39;re both plausible, right?
